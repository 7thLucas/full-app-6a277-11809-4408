# Interview Insight Plus — Product Overview

## What It Is
Interview Insight Plus is an AI-powered interview video analysis platform. Hiring teams upload recorded interview videos; the platform automatically transcribes them, extracts structured candidate signals, and surfaces actionable insights — so teams spend their time deciding, not reviewing footage.

## Users & Audience
- **Primary:** Recruiting teams and talent acquisition professionals at companies that conduct structured or semi-structured interviews.
- **Secondary:** Hiring managers who need consolidated candidate summaries without sitting through full recordings.

## Core Capabilities
1. **Upload & Auto-Transcribe** — Drop video files directly into the platform; AI transcription with speaker labels and timestamps is ready in minutes.
2. **Analyze Candidate Signals** — Automatic extraction of key moments: technical depth, communication clarity, cultural alignment, and decision-making patterns.
3. **Compare & Decide** — Side-by-side candidate profiles, searchable interview library, and a unified dashboard that surfaces hiring patterns across the pipeline.

## Positioning
Interview Insight Plus turns raw interview footage into hiring intelligence. It reduces per-recruiter review time by ~65%, extracts 4× more structured data points than manual review, and surfaces consistent candidate success factors at 87% pattern-match accuracy — saving 10+ hours per recruiter per week.

## Brand & Tone
- **Voice:** Precise, confident, data-forward. Never hype-heavy.
- **Primary color:** #0066cc (deep blue). Accent: #00d4ff (electric cyan).
- **Feel:** Professional analytics tool, not a consumer app.

## Strategic Principles
- Insights must be actionable, not decorative.
- Every feature reduces friction in the hiring decision loop.
- Bias reduction through systematic, structured analysis is a first-class goal.
