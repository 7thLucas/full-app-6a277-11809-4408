# Interview Insight Plus

## What It Is
An AI-powered platform for analyzing company interview videos. It extracts key insights, evaluates candidate communication patterns, and turns raw footage into structured hiring intelligence.

## Target Users
- HR teams at companies
- Hiring managers

## Core Value Proposition
Transform raw interview footage into actionable, structured hiring data — eliminating hours of manual review and bringing consistency and objectivity to candidate evaluation.

## Key Features
- Upload and manage interview video recordings
- AI-driven transcription and content extraction
- Candidate communication pattern analysis (clarity, confidence, structure, pacing)
- Insight dashboards per candidate and per role
- Side-by-side candidate comparison
- Structured hiring intelligence reports
- Search and filter across interviews
- Team collaboration and annotation tools

## Brand & Tone
- Professional, data-driven, trustworthy
- Clear and precise language — no fluff
- Empowers hiring teams with confidence
- Name: Interview Insight Plus

## Anti-References
- Avoid overly casual or consumer-app aesthetics
- Avoid cluttered dashboards with too many widgets
- No gimmicky AI language ("magic", "supercharge") — keep it grounded and credible
